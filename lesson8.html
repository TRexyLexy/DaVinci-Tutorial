Lesson 8: Batching

Step 1 of 10

Batch Edit

Batching is an essential tool for effeciency used in DaVinci.

This tool can be used in several different ways, such as batch editing the status of orders, users, ect.

Batch Edit

    Highlight the orders you would like to change by clicking on one then pressing shift and clicking on the last one.


    To batch edit, simply click on the 3 dots at the top of the tool bar.
