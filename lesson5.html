Lesson 5: Transportation Management

Step 1 of 10

What Is Transportation Management?

It’s the process of planning, executing, and controlling the movement of goods from origin to destination efficiently and cost-effectively.

BRW’s Services: Trucking, Logistics, Warehousing, and Fulfillment.

Key Operations & Terms

  • Terminal Management: Set up and control locations.
  • User Access: Manage permissions.
  • Goods Movement: Pick Up, Delivery, Move, Return.
  • WMS: Manages Receiving/Shipping.
  • TMS: Manages Dispatch, Loads, Shipments.

Creating Warehouse Terminals

Steps:

  1. Go to Dashboard > Dropdown > Settings > Warehouse – Terminal.
  2. Click Insert to create a new terminal.

Managing User Access

Steps:

  1. Go to Settings > Security > Users.
  2. Select a user and manage their terminal access.
  3. Click Save after changes.

Account Linking

Purpose: Accounts link warehousing and transportation together.

Steps:

  1. Dashboard > Business Partners > Accounts.
  2. Click Insert to add a new account.

Creating Loads

Steps:

  1. Dashboard > Transportation > Loads.
  2. Click Insert and fill in load details (partners, origin, dates, cost).

Creating Pickups & Deliveries

Pickup:

  1. Go to Receiving Orders.
  2. Select an order, click Actions, then Create Pickup.

Delivery:

  1. Go to Shipping Orders.
  2. Select an order, click Actions, then Create Delivery.

Dispatch Management

Steps:

  1. Dashboard > Transportation > Dispatch.
  2. View, edit, or create outbound shipments from this section.

Advanced Dispatch Tools

  • Manage Loads: View/edit/create loads directly in Dispatch.
  • Auto Refresh: Enable real-time updates every few minutes.
  • Filters: Select Drivers, Shippers, Carriers, Equipment to manage views.

Shipping Details

Steps:

  1. Dashboard > Transportation > Shipments.
  2. View shipment status, documentation, and update details as needed.
