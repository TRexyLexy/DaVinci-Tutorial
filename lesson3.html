Lesson 3: Order Management

1 of 2

Receiving Inventory

To create a receiving order, navigate to the dropdown menu (three lines) in the top left corner.
Select "Orders" then "Receiving Orders".

From the menu showing all receiving orders, click the "Insert" button (green plus), and then select "Create New Receipt".

Fill out the necessary fields. A Transaction Number will be created after saving.

You can create new carriers or suppliers using the insert button next to the desired field.

To add items, click "Line Entries" on the left sidebar under "General", then click the insert button. A pop-up menu will appear where you can input the item code and planned quantity.

To print a pre-receipt or receipt, upload the necessary documents. Click "Action" in the top right corner, then "Print" to see various printing options.

Shipping Orders

The initial steps for shipping orders are similar to receiving orders.
Instead of "Receiving Orders", choose "Shipping Orders" from the dropdown menu.

The "create order" menu for shipping is very similar to that for receiving.
Adding items to a shipping order is similar to receiving orders.

For "System Selected Locations", enter the item code, unit price, ordered quantity, and shipped quantity. DaVinci will automatically calculate quantity and weight.

Adding line items as "Manual Selected Locations" allows you to search from existing item options.

After completing your shipping order, several printing options are available.
